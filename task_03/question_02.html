Star Wars Film Locations Around the World

1. Tunisia - Tatooine

Tunisia was used to film various scenes set on Tatooine, a desert planet.

2. Norway - Hoth

Scenes of the ice planet Hoth in The Empire Strikes Back were shot in Norway.

3. Ireland - Ahch-To

Skellig Michael in Ireland doubled as Ahch-To, Luke Skywalker's hideout in The Last Jedi.

4. Italy - Naboo

The Royal Palace of Caserta in Italy was used for the interior shots of the Royal Palace of Naboo.

5. Guatemala - Yavin 4

The ancient Mayan ruins of Tikal in Guatemala served as the Rebel Base on Yavin 4.

6. Iceland - Eadu and Hoth

Iceland's dramatic landscapes were used to depict the planet Eadu in Rogue One and also scenes of Hoth.

7. United Arab Emirates - Jakku

The desert of the United Arab Emirates was the backdrop for the planet Jakku in The Force Awakens.
